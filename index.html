FAQ
What is Early Bird?
Dealers post what they're bringing to the market. You buy the best stuff before the general public gets in. No more showing up at dawn and hoping.
What does it cost?
Free during early access. Eventually it'll be like buying an early entry ticket to the market — a small fee to get first crack at everything.
How do I pay?
Directly with the dealer — Venmo, Zelle, or cash at the booth. Early Bird doesn't handle payments.
Is this affiliated with the markets?
No. Early Bird is independent. Dealers choose to list here on their own.
What markets are listed?
Starting with markets in the LA area — more coming soon.
Early Bird is not affiliated with or endorsed by any market listed here. All listings are posted by independent dealers.

One photo and a price, minimum. You can add a title, condition notes, and whether the price is firm.
