FAQ
What is Early Bird?
Early Bird lets you see and buy items from flea market dealers before the market opens to the public. Think of it as a private preview.
Is this free?
Yes. Registering and browsing is completely free for buyers.
How do I pay?
You pay the dealer directly at the market when you pick up your item. Early Bird doesn't handle payments.
Is this affiliated with the markets?
No. Early Bird is an independent platform. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by any market listed here.
What markets are listed?
We're starting with LA-area markets like Downtown Modernism, Rose Bowl, and Long Beach. More coming soon.
Early Bird is not affiliated with or endorsed by any market listed here. All listings are posted by independent dealers.
Design Notes
