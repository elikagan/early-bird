FAQ
What is Early Bird?
Early Bird lets you see what dealers are bringing to flea markets and buy items before the general public gets access. Think of it as a preview sale — you shop early, then pick up at the market.
Is this free?
Yes. Early Bird is free for buyers. You just pay the dealer directly for any items you purchase.
How do I pay?
You pay the dealer directly at the market when you pick up your item. Early Bird doesn't process payments — we just connect you with the seller.
Is this affiliated with the markets?
No. Early Bird is an independent platform. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by any market listed here.
What markets are listed?
We're starting with LA-area markets — Downtown Modernism, Rose Bowl, Long Beach, and more. New markets are added regularly.
