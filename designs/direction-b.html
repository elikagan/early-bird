Questions
What is Early Bird?
Early Bird lets you see what dealers are bringing to flea markets and buy items before the market opens to the public.
Is this free?
Yes. Browsing and buying on Early Bird is completely free for buyers.
How do I pay?
You pay the dealer directly at the market when you pick up your item. Early Bird doesn't process payments.
Is this affiliated with the markets?
No. Early Bird is an independent platform. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by any market listed here.
What markets are listed?
We're starting with LA-area markets like Downtown Modernism, Rose Bowl, and Long Beach. More coming soon.
